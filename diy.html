DIY & Budget Builds

Low-cost options you can make or adapt with basic tools—great for starting without big spends.

Growing

Kitchen herb gardens (indoor)

Start with compact, quick growers (basil, chives, parsley). Bright windows + well-draining pots are key.

UMN Extension — Growing herbs UMN Extension — Herbs indoors Master Gardeners — Indoor herb ideas
Raised beds (outdoor)

Raised beds warm quickly, drain well, and simplify soil management. Use untreated wood or safe repurposed materials.

Composting

Kitchen composters (indoor)

Indoor worm bins turn scraps into rich castings; they’re compact and odor-managed when built correctly.

Garden composters (outdoor)

Wire mesh or pallet bins cost little, breathe well, and scale easily for yard waste.
