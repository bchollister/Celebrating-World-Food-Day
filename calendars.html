Regional notes

Quick guide
  • West & Southwest: Cool crops fall–spring; warm crops late spring.
  • Pacific Northwest: Mild summers; short-season varieties.
  • Mountain & High Desert: Expect swings; row covers help.
  • Midwest & Northeast: Start seeds indoors to maximize summer.
  • Southeast & Gulf: Long seasons; time cool crops for fall/winter.
  • SoCal coastal: Many greens & herbs year-round; summer shade.
